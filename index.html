Experience

Responsive Web Designer

2023 - Present

A Responsive Web Designer is a digital architect who specializes in creating websites that adapt seamlessly to various screen sizes and devices. They possess a unique blend of design sensibility and technical expertise, ensuring that the user experience remains optimal whether viewed on a desktop computer, tablet, or smartphone.

These skilled professionals are masters of CSS, HTML, and responsive design frameworks, enabling them to craft visually appealing and functional websites that automatically adjust layout and content to suit the user's device. Responsive Web Designers prioritize user accessibility, ensuring that the website remains user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing across the ever-expanding spectrum of devices and screen resolutions.

Full Stack Web Developer

2023 - Present

A Full Stack Web Developer is a versatile and well-rounded professional who possesses the skills and expertise to work on both the front end and back end of web applications. They are like the architects of the digital world, capable of designing, building, and maintaining every aspect of a website or web application.

On the front end, Full Stack Developers are adept at creating intuitive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They focus on ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience. Meanwhile, on the back end, they manage databases, servers, and server-side programming languages like Python, Ruby, or PHP, handling the logic and functionality that powers the website.
